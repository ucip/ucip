    <h3 class="home-h3">Finding the UCiP</h3>
    <p class="text-justify">The UCiP will be held in McKenny Hall on the Eastern Michigan University campus. The address for McKenny Hall is:</p>
    <address>
      McKenny Hall<br>
      W. Cross St.<br>
      Ypsilanti, MI 48197
    </address>
