EMU Undergraduate Conference in Philosophy

Welcome to the main page for Eastern Michigan University's Undergraduate Conference in Philosophy (UCiP)! The Winter 2017 conference has concluded, but we'll be back in Winter 2018! The Call for Papers will be sent out in early November.

On this website, you can check out last year's submission guidelines, advice for speakers, and advice for commentators. Don't forget to check out the EMU Undergraduate Journal, Acta Cogitata! Several papers from the conference are published in this journal each year.

Additionally, you can review the Winter 2017 UCiP Schedule to get a feel for how the conference flows, and see what's happened at past conferences.

Note: The UCiP Schedule is announced shortly after the paper submission deadline has passed each year.

Finally, you can find directions to campus and our campus map, nearby hotels and restaurants, and learn about the Public Transport System (AATA, or "The Ride") in the Ypsi/AnnArbor area.
