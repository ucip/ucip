EMU Undergraduate Conference in Philosophy

Welcome to the main page for Eastern Michigan University's Undergraduate Conference in Philosophy (UCiP)! UCiP will be held in McKenny Hall on the EMU Campus on Saturday and Sunday, March 4th and 5th, 2017.

On this website, you can find guidelines for submitting your paper, advice on how to present your paper at the UCiP, and advice on how to comment on other's papers. Don't forget to check out the EMU Undergraduate Journal, Acta Cogitata! Several papers from the conference are published in this journal each year.

Additionally, you can review the 2016 UCiP Schedule to get a feel for how the conference flows, and see what's happened at past conferences.

Note: The 2017 UCiP Schedule will be announced shortly after the paper submission deadline (Jan. 10, 2017) has passed.

Finally, you can find out how to get to, and navigate arround, the EMU campus, and find local accommodations and restaurants in Ypsilanti, and learn about the Public Transport System (AATA, or "The Ride") in the Ypsi/AnnArbor area.
