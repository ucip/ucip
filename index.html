EMU Undergraduate Conference in Philosophy

Welcome to the main page for Eastern Michigan University's Undergraduate Conference in Philosophy (UCiP)! The Winter 2018 conference will be held Saturday and Sunday, March 3rd and 4th, 2018. Review our Call for Papers!

Note: Papers selected for presentation will be announced by the end of January. The 2018 UCiP Schedule will be available shortly thereafter.

On this website, you can check out our advice for speakers, advice for commentators, and the EMU Undergraduate Journal, Acta Cogitata! Several of the top papers from the conference are published in this journal each year.

Additionally, you can review the Winter 2017 UCiP Schedule, look through past programs, notes, and keynote speakers, and see what's happened at past conferences to get a feel for how UCiP operates.

Finally, you can find directions to campus and our campus map, nearby hotels and restaurants, and learn about the Public Transport System (AATA, or "The Ride") in the Ypsi/AnnArbor area.
